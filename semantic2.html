header element

Introduction for the whole page or individual sections,article,nav,aside elements.
Typically contains site name, logo, navigation. Does not have to be at the beginning of page.

Example:


<header>
<h1> The importance of being earnest</h1>
<h3 > A quest for truth and Beauty</h3 >
<p > The play was written in 1895 by playwright Oscar wilde</p >
</header >

Footer element

Includes typical footer information like authoring, copyrights, contact information and a footer menu.

<footer>
<p >Written by OScar Wilde</p >
<p >Contact information: <a href=mailto:ocar@wilde.com>oscar@wilde.com</a ></p >
</footer >

nav element:

Navigation links for the document. A page can have more than one <nav >element like
table of contents, horizantal navigation in header and footer navigation.
